Verity
by Colleen Hoover · 2026-01-28
★★★☆☆

The setup made for a juicy story with a twisted family inviting a stranger to discover their secrets. The character building let the plot down and unfortunately, as happens with one too many books of this thriller genre, the reveal felt wholly unsatisfying, leaving me with a sour taste for what could've been a richer, more well thought out story.
