Books in 2025

Butter by Asako Yuzuki · 2025-12-26
★★★★

What a delicious book. One of the best ones I read in 2025, it hit all the right notes - emotional, dramatic, thrilling and oh what fun to read. Manako Kaji, a prisoner suspected to have murdered three of her lovers, had me at hello. Rika Machado, the protagonist, a journalist hell-bent on getting out the facts, felt very relatable. It was such a delight flipping through the pages, experiencing the journey with her, all to a very satisfying, scrumptious conclusion.
